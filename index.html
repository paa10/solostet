About

Solomon Tetteh was born on 13 July, 1992 is a Software Developer and a Teacher. I was born in Aburi and started my early child at Suhum in the Eastern Region of Ghana. I went to Suhum Anglican JHS then continue to Suhum Senior High/Technical School for General Science in 2013, further to Accra Technical University for HND Science Laboratory Technology in 2017. I also have British and New Zealand Certificates including Postgraduate Diploma (PGD) in Clinical Genetics from Coventry University, Bsc. Ai Development from Waikato University New Zealand,Advanced Diploma in Laboratory Technician and Services from Apex Learning, UK, Health and Safety and more others.
